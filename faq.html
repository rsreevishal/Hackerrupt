FAQ

  • How Much Will It Cost?

    Rs. 300 per head.

  • Why do we have to pay Rs.300?

    Your meals for the weekend will be provided by us and there’ll be plenty of snacks and drinks to keep you energized.We are also offering some exciting pack of goodies!

  • Will Food Be Provided?

    Of course! Yes. An amazing palate of breakfast, lunch and dinner will be provided during the hackathon along with tidbits of snacks to keep you going!

  • Beverages?

    We'll be sure to keep you hydrated and refreshed with soft drinks and other energy drinks throughout the day and to get you through even the crazy hours of midnight!

  • Number Of Paticipants

    The number of participants in a team can vary from 1 to a maximum of 4. Please make sure you have the right number of members in your team!

  • Who Can Participate?

    Any student pursuing an Engineering UnderGrad from any department with a reasonable knowledge of technology can participate.

  • What Do You Need To Bring?

    Students are advised to bring their own laptops, pendrives or any other equipments and gadgets you might require. Charging ports will be provided at the venue.

  • Will Wifi Be Provided?

    Yes! You'll be provided with one WiFi connection per person upon commencement of the event. Remember, you can connect only one device at a time. Although, you can still use your own Internet or bring a dongle. 

  • Last Date For Registration

    March 10 is the last date for registration and submission of abstracts. Shortlisted teams will be intimated through Email. 

  • What Is The Judging Criteria?

    The judging criteria will be specified at the time of the event.

  • What Do I Get Out Of This Hackathon?

    You'll get to experience a whole new level of excitement, fun and learning put together through this hackathon along with intern oppurtunities. Plus there'll be exciting awards and cash prizes to look forward to!

  • Who Do I Contact If I Have Doubts?

    Please refer the contact section of this website to contact our ACE team for any information and doubts regarding the hackathon.
